### Human-Resource-Analytics-dashboard

This README provides business-focused analysis, key insights, and metrics definitions for the dashboards included in this repository. The goal is to help hiring managers, recruiters, and collaborators understand analytical thinking, KPI design, and impact-driven approach as a Business Analyst.

#Objective:

To analyze workforce patterns, improve employee retention, and strengthen hiring strategies.

#Key Metrics & KPIs:

>Headcount Trend (Monthly/Quarterly)

>Attrition Rate & Retention Ratio

>Hiring Funnel Metrics (Applications → Interviews → Offers)

>Time-to-Hire & Cost-per-Hire

>Diversity Metrics (Gender, Age, Department)

>Employee Performance Distribution


#Insights Provided:

>Identified departments with >15% attrition requiring policy interventions.

>Hiring funnel showed bottlenecks at screening and technical rounds.

>Diversity analysis highlighted underrepresented groups.

>Headcount vs productivity trend helped in workload balancing.


#Business Impact:

>Reduces hiring costs through optimized recruitment funnels.

>Enhances retention by identifying drivers of employee exits.

>Supports workforce planning and skill‑gap identification.


#How to Use These Dashboards:

1.Open the .twb / .twbx files using Tableau Desktop or Tableau Public.

2.Review metrics & insights listed above while exploring the dashboard.

3.Use the dashboards to extract business insights and re-run analysis with updated data.
